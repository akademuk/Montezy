About Us

Who We Are

Montezy GmbH is a Swiss fintech company dedicated to delivering financial services that are secure, transparent, and global. Our mission is to empower individuals, freelancers, and businesses to manage money and digital assets with confidence — without borders.

Our Journey

Montezy GmbH is currently in the process of becoming a member of VQF (Verein zur Qualitätssicherung von Finanzdienstleistungen). This regulatory journey ensures our services are supervised, compliant, and held to the highest Swiss and European standards.

Our Vision

To become a trusted bridge between traditional finance and the digital economy, guided by Swiss compliance and global innovation.

At Montezy, compliance is not an afterthought — it's our foundation.

Regulatory Pathway:

Montezy GmbH is in the process of becoming a member of VQF, operating under Swiss AMLA supervision.

AML & KYC:

We apply industry-leading onboarding, monitoring, and transaction screening standards.

Safeguarding:

All client funds are held securely with regulated Swiss and EEA banking partners, always segregated and protected.

Transparency:

Crypto Oversight:
